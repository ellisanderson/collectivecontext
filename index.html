Collective Context

is a participatory site-spotting project that aggregates contextual experiences of forgotten or easily ignored places. By collecting immediate impressions of essential elements within an environment, Collective Context visions and versions a location from those who traffic the space. The goal of the project is twofold: to define and redefine these spaces according to shifting and multitudinous interpretation, and to call attention to assumptions of function, to question the “toolness” of a given location.
